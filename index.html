Legal Policies

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Time Xchange (Pty) Ltd ("Time X", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Time X mobile app and website at timexchange.co.za.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete your profile, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Profile information and service descriptions
  • Communication preferences and in-app messages
  • Subscription and purchase status (processed through Apple App Store or Google Play; we do not store payment card details)
  • Location data when you choose to share it for nearby services
  • Photos you upload for your profile

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Manage memberships and in-app purchases
  • Send technical notices, support messages, and push notifications you opt into
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Protect against fraudulent or illegal activity

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us operate the app (such as hosting and authentication), when required by law, or with your consent.

Subscriptions and Payments

Auto-renewable subscriptions are billed through Apple or Google. Payment processing is subject to the respective store's terms. See Auto-Renewable Subscriptions for plan details.

Your Choices

You may update your profile, manage notification preferences in the app, or contact us to request account or data deletion at support@timexchange.co.za.

Contact

Questions about this policy: support@timexchange.co.za

Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

Last updated: July 2026

Time X offers optional auto-renewable memberships that renew monthly until cancelled. Payment is charged to your Apple ID or Google Play account at confirmation of purchase. Subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device account settings.
